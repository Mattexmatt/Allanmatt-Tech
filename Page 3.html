Betting Strategies

Learn proven strategies to improve your betting performance and maximize profits.

Value Betting Strategy +

Value betting involves identifying bets where the probability of an outcome is higher than what the odds suggest. Our AI helps find these value opportunities by comparing our predicted probabilities with bookmaker odds.

  • Focus on matches where our confidence score is 70%+
  • Look for odds discrepancies across bookmakers
  • Bet only when you have a clear edge
Bankroll Management +

Proper bankroll management is crucial for long-term success in sports betting. We recommend the following approach:

  • Never bet more than 1-2% of your bankroll on a single bet
  • Adjust stake sizes based on confidence levels
  • Keep detailed records of all your bets
  • Withdraw profits periodically
Live Betting Tactics +

Live betting offers unique opportunities for informed bettors. Our real-time algorithms help identify these situations:

  • Look for matches where the pre-game favorite is losing but dominating statistically
  • Target over/under markets when the game pace changes
  • Watch for key player substitutions that change team dynamics
